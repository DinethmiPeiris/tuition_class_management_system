Features

Everything you need in one place

Designed for both teachers and students, TMS makes managing tuition classes simple and effective.

Class Timetable

View your weekly class schedule, subject timings, and room details — all organised and easy to follow.

Online Exams

Take MCQ and essay-based exams directly online. MCQs are auto-marked and results are shown instantly.

Progress Tracking

See your scores, pass/fail status, and performance history. Never lose track of your academic journey.

Practice Questions

Teachers can add MCQ and essay questions for students to practice and strengthen their understanding.

Announcements

Stay updated with exam reminders, class cancellations, and important notices posted by your teacher.

Secure Role Access

Separate secure portals for teachers and students, so everyone sees only what is relevant to them.
